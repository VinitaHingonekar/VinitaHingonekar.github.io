Just Shoot?

3D FPS Game in Unity


Project Overview


"Just Shoot?" is a 3D first-person shooter game developed in Unity, featuring various gameplay mechanics and systems to enhance player engagement and experienceme.



Key Features


  1. FPS Camera
    • Implemented a first-person camera for immersive gameplay.

  2. Player Movement:
    • Added basic movements like walking, sprinting, crouching, and jumping to provide fluid player control.

  3. Shooting Mechanic:
    • Developed a shooting system with 6 different types of guns, each with unique firing rates and reload animations.

  4. Custom Weapons:
    • Designed all gun models in Blender and integrated them into Unity.

  5. Enemy AI:
    • Created two types of enemies: one with melee attacks and one that shoots from a distance, both with AI behaviors to follow and attack the player.
    • Used NavMeshSurface to enable enemy navigation and pathfinding.
    • Created a dynamic spawn manager to randomly generate enemies on the map, ensuring varied gameplay.

  6. Visual Effects:
    • Added animations for shooting and reloading to enhance gameplay realism.
    • Used Unity's particle system to create shooting effects for a visually engaging experience.

  7. Shop System:
    • Implemented an in-game shop for players to purchase guns and health using in-game currency.

  8. Health System:
    • Developed comprehensive health systems for both the player and enemies.

  9. Scoring System:
    • Designed a scoring and money mechanism where different types of enemies provide different scores and money.


Skills Demonstrated:

  1. Game Design:
    • Conceptualizing and implementing game mechanics and UI/UX elements.

  2. Unity Development:
    • Utilizing Unity's features to create interactive and engaging gameplay.

  3. 3D Modelling:
    • Experience with Blender for 3D modeling and asset creation.
