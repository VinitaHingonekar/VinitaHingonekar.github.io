Chamber Of Questions

2D Rapid Quiz Game in Unity


Project Overview


"Chamber of Questions" is a 2D quiz game developed in Unity, featuring 8 quizzes based on the Harry Potter movie series. The game is designed to test how much of a Potterhead a player is through a series of timed questions.



Key Features


  1. 8 Themed Quizzes:
    • Each quiz focuses on one of the eight Harry Potter movies, allowing players to choose their preferred quiz from a main menu.

  2. Timed Questions:
    • Players have 5 seconds to answer each question, adding a level of challenge and excitement.

  3. Instant Feedback:
    • If a player selects the wrong answer, the correct answer is displayed for 2 seconds to enhance learning.

  4. Scoring System:
    • Scores are shown as a percentage throughout the quiz, with a final score displayed at the end to track performance.
