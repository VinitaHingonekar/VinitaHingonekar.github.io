About ME

From a young age, I have always been fascinated by the world of games, spending countless hours playing and exploring various genres. I loved expressing my creativity through painting, sketching, and building new things from scratch. Little did I know that these childhood passions would eventually shape my career. With a degree in computer science, I developed a deep interest in coding and web development. That’s when I discovered the field of game development and began learning it as a hobby.

After completing my studies, my passion for gaming eventually led me to immerse myself in game development, merging my childhood love for games and creativity into a fulfilling profession. Today, I am wholeheartedly dedicated to turning my lifelong dream into reality by using my technical expertise and creative flair to create captivating games that connect with players around the world.
